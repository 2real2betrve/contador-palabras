How to use the word counter?

Simply type or paste your text in the text area above. Our counter will automatically calculate:

  • Words: Total words in your text
  • Characters: All characters including spaces
  • No spaces: Characters without counting spaces
  • Sentences: Based on periods, question marks and exclamation points
  • Paragraphs: Text blocks separated by blank lines
  • Reading time: Estimated at 200 words per minute

  • ✅ Creating perfect SEO meta descriptions
  • ✅ Checking article and blog length
  • ✅ Preparing speeches with estimated time
