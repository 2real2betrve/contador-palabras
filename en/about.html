What is TextCounter.Online?

TextCounter.Online is a free and easy-to-use tool that allows you to count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in real-time. Designed for writers, students, marketing professionals, and anyone who needs to analyze text quickly.

Key Features

  • ✅ Real-time word counter: Count words as you type
  • ✅ Character counter: With and without spaces
  • ✅ Sentence and paragraph analysis: Structure your text better
  • ✅ Estimated reading time: Based on 200 words per minute
  • ✅ Social media limits: Check if your text meets Twitter, Instagram, TikTok limits and more
  • ✅ 100% private: Your text never leaves your browser
  • ✅ No registration: Use the tool instantly
  • ✅ Available in English and Spanish

Who is this tool for?

  • Students: Verify that your essays meet word limits
  • Writers: Analyze the structure of your texts
  • Marketing professionals: Optimize content for social media
  • Bloggers and content creators: Ensure ideal length for SEO
  • Professionals: Prepare speeches and presentations

Why choose TextCounter.Online?

Unlike other tools, TextCounter.Online is completely free, requires no registration, and respects your privacy. All processing is done in your browser, so your texts are never sent to any server.

Privacy and Security

Your privacy is our priority. We do not store, save, or transmit the text you enter. Read our Privacy Policy for more information.
