Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Local Text Processing

At WordCount Pro, we value your privacy above all else. No text you type, paste, or upload into our tool is ever sent to our servers. All word counting, keyword density analysis, and readability formulas are executed locally within your own browser via JavaScript.

2. Analytics Data Collection

To improve user experience, we use web analytics services (such as Google Analytics). These services may collect anonymous information about:

  • Pages visited within our site.
  • Time spent on the application.
  • Type of browser and device used.

3. Advertising and Cookies (AdSense)

To keep the tool free, we display ads through the Google AdSense program. Google and its partners use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to reach out via our Contact page.
