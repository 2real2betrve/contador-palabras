1. Information We Collect

TextCounter.Online is a free tool that works entirely in your browser. We do not store, save, or transmit any text you enter into the tool. All processing is done locally on your device.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use Google AdSense to display ads on our site. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your interests. You can learn more about how Google uses your information by visiting Google's ad policies.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This tool collects anonymous information such as pages visited, time spent, and approximate geographic location. We do not identify individual users.

4. Your Rights

You can:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Use the tool without providing any personal information
  • Opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google's ad settings

5. Data Security

Your text never leaves your browser. We have no access to the data you enter in the word counter tool.

6. External Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We recommend you review this page periodically.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through our contact page.

9. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Port your data to another service

Since we do not collect identifiable personal data, these rights primarily apply to information collected by Google Analytics and AdSense.
