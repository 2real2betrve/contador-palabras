About Us

Our Mission

At WordCount Pro, our mission is to provide writers, students, and digital marketing professionals with the fastest, most accurate, and most beautiful text analysis tool on the internet. We believe that counting words shouldn't be a boring task, but an integrated experience that enhances your productivity.

Why Choose Us?

Unlike basic counters, we have built a true "Productivity SaaS" that operates 100% in your browser (without sacrificing your privacy by sending data to external servers). We integrate real-time analytics, scientific readability formulas (adapted Flesch-Kincaid), and SEO density to give you full control over your content.

Privacy by Design

Built with the latest web technologies, WordCount Pro ensures that your confidential documents never leave your computer. All complex calculations of text algorithms are executed natively in your device's RAM memory.
